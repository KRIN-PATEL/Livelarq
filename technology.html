Inside the Technology: The Way LARQ Maintains Your Water Pure

The Science of UV-C Purification

UV-C light has long been recognized for its germicidal properties. It offers an effective solution to eliminate harmful bacteria, viruses, and microorganisms. LARQ integrates this powerful technology into its self-cleaning bottles, ensuring every sip is safe and clean.

UV-C works by disrupting the DNA of pathogens, rendering them inactive. The PureVis™ technology precisely purifies water with minimal effort, making it convenient for daily use at home, in the office, or outdoors.

Health and Environmental Benefits of LARQ Technology

LARQ offers more than just clean water. Its self-cleaning technology reduces reliance on disposable filters and cleaning chemicals, promoting an eco-friendly approach to hydration.

Built with durable, food-grade stainless steel, the LARQ bottle is designed to last. It minimizes plastic waste, making it a sustainable alternative to disposable bottles. By choosing LARQ, you contribute to reducing environmental pollution.

Additionally, LARQ supports global water initiatives through its membership in the 1% for the Planet network. A portion of every sale goes towards nonprofits focused on clean water access and environmental conservation.

How Does LARQ's Self-Cleaning Technology Work?

The self-cleaning technology in LARQ bottles uses a PureVis™ UV-C LED light, housed in the cap. This light activates with the touch of a button, purifying water in just 60 seconds.

The bottle also features an automatic cleaning mode, which activates every two hours to ensure continuous cleanliness. This hands-free feature makes it ideal for active lifestyles.

Powered by a rechargeable battery, the LARQ bottle can last up to a month on a single charge. Its waterproof design ensures durability, making it perfect for both everyday use and outdoor adventures.
