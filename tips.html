Essential Cleaning and Maintenance Advice for Your LARQ Bottle

Tips to Keep Your LARQ Bottle in Top Condition

Why Proper Maintenance Extends the Life of Your LARQ Bottle

Your LARQ bottle is an investment in clean, convenient hydration. With its innovative PureVis™ UV-C technology, the LARQ bottle offers a self-cleaning solution. This guide will walk you through easy maintenance practices and efficient cleaning techniques.

Easy Steps for Weekly and Daily Maintenance

Maintaining your LARQ bottle doesn’t have to be complicated. Weekly maintenance involves filling the bottle with warm water and mild dish soap. Use a soft bottle brush to gently scrub the interior, and rinse thoroughly. Avoid abrasive sponges and dishwashers.

For daily care, rinse your bottle with warm water after each use, especially if you used it for beverages other than water. Leave the cap off while drying to maintain airflow.

How to Clean the LARQ Bottle Cap Safely Without Damaging the Technology

The LARQ bottle cap contains PureVis™ UV-C LED technology, which requires special care. Avoid submerging the cap in water. Instead, gently wipe the exterior with a damp cloth and clean around sensitive areas like the USB port with a dry cotton swab.

If you encounter stubborn spots, a paste of baking soda and water can be used to clean the bottle's exterior without damaging the finish.

Troubleshooting Common Issues and Ensuring Top Performance

  • UV-C Light Fails to Activate: Recharge the battery fully and ensure the USB port is clean and free from debris.
  • Bottle Develops an Odor: Fill the bottle with warm water and a tablespoon of baking soda. Let it sit for a few hours, then rinse thoroughly.
  • Cap Doesn’t Charge Properly: Use the original USB cable and check the USB port for dust or damage. Avoid liquid cleaners near the port.
